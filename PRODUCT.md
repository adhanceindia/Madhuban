# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Prospective resort guests, families, wedding planners, event hosts, and local visitors evaluating Madhuban Garden Resort on mobile and desktop. They need to understand the property, explore rooms and venues, and confidently move toward a booking or inquiry.

## Product Purpose

Present Madhuban Garden Resort as the most peaceful and lush green hospitality destination in Agar Malwa District. The site should build trust through strong property imagery, make the wedding venue the clearest premium offering, and help visitors discover rooms, dining, celebrations, and nearby attractions without friction.

## Brand Personality

Lush, peaceful, and warmly premium. The experience should feel natural, spacious, emotionally inviting, and distinctly connected to the garden resort rather than corporate hospitality.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic hotel templates, corporate layouts, obvious AI aesthetics, purple gradients, sterile minimalism, cramped compositions, and visual treatments that weaken the nature-led identity.

## Design Principles

1. Let real resort imagery and greenery carry the atmosphere.
2. Keep every screen airy, calm, and easy to scan on mobile first.
3. Give weddings the strongest emotional and visual emphasis.
4. Use consistent, legible hierarchy so key actions remain immediately clear.
5. Preserve a warm local character while maintaining production-grade polish.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain strong text contrast over photography, visible keyboard focus, semantic structure, descriptive image alternatives, responsive layouts, and reduced-motion support where animation is used. Target WCAG 2.1 AA conventions for public-facing interactions.
